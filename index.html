Welcome To ICETIGT-2024

The International Conference on Energy Transition and Innovation in Green Technology, jointly organized by Madan Mohan Malviya University of Technology in Gorakhpur, India, and the Asian Institute of Technology in Bangkok, Thailand, stands as a beacon for fostering global collaboration and knowledge exchange in the realm of sustainable energy and green technology. This conference serves as a dynamic platform where leading experts, researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers converge to deliberate on cutting-edge advancements in energy transition and innovation. With a shared commitment to addressing the challenges of climate change and promoting sustainable development, the event showcases groundbreaking research and technological innovations that pave the way for a greener and more sustainable future.

Participants can expect a diverse range of presentations and discussions covering topics such as renewable energy sources, energy-efficient technologies, smart grids, environmental policies, and emerging trends in green technology. The collaborative efforts between Madan Mohan Malviya University of Technology and the Asian Institute of Technology underscore the global significance of the conference, fostering cross-cultural insights and collaborative initiatives to accelerate the transition towards a more sustainable and environmentally friendly energy landscape. This conference not only facilitates the exchange of knowledge but also inspires collective action towards a cleaner and more sustainable planet.

All accepted & successfully presented papers of the conference will be published in International Energy Journal Terms & Conditions of Journal will ne as per rules of Publication.
